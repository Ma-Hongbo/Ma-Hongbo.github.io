Hongbo Ma · 马洪博

B.S. Candidate, Tsinghua University (CS + Economics & Management Minor)

I am an undergraduate researcher from Xinya College and Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. My research focuses on computer vision, generative models, and intelligent agent systems.

Building on these areas, I aim to develop embodied agents that can perceive, reason, and act in real-world environments, and further explore scientific agents for solving complex real-world problems.

Current interests: visual representation learning, world modeling, efficient agent reasoning, and real-world generalization.
